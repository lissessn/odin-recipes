Tiramisu Recipe

Description

Tiramisu is a traditional Italian sweet dish, made with coffee, sponge, egg yolks, mascarpone, and cocoa.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup cocoa powder, plus more for dusting
  • 1 cup brewed espresso (optional to add a few tablespoons of dark rum)
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extracts
  • 5 large egg yolks, colds
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 16 ounces mascarpone cheese, chilled
  • 1.75 cups heavy cream, chilled
  • 28 hard ladyfinger cookies
