Tiramisu Recipe

Description

Tiramisu is a traditional Italian sweet dish, made with coffee, sponge, egg yolks, mascarpone, and cocoa.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup cocoa powder, plus more for dusting
  • 1 cup brewed espresso (optional to add a few tablespoons of dark rum)
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extracts
  • 5 large egg yolks, colds
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 16 ounces mascarpone cheese, chilled
  • 1.75 cups heavy cream, chilled
  • 28 hard ladyfinger cookies

Steps

  1. Whisk together the cocoa powder, espresso and vanilla in a bowl and set aside.
  2. In a mixer or using a hand mixer, beat the egg yolks and sugar until pale and thick, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add in the salt, mascarpone cheese and continue to whip until smooth.
  4. Add the chilled heavy cream and continue to whip, until light, creamy, smooth and can hold a soft peak
  5. Dunk each ladyfinger in the espresso mixture to absorb the liquid and start to line a 7x11 oval baking dish with the cookies.
  6. Top the first layer of cookies with ½ of the whipped mascarpone mixture and use the back of a metal spoon to spread it into an even layer.
  7. Dust with cocoa powder. Repeat for another layer and then finish by dusting with cocoa powder on top.
  8. Cover with plastic wrap and chill for at least 2 hours before serving.

Notes

  1. ALL the ingredients for the filling must be cold
  2. Just a quick dunk of a ladyfinger cookie will do - nothing more than 1-2 seconds!
  3. Make sure you leave time to rest the tiramisu.
